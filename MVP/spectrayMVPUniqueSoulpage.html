Unique Soul

2016 | Country: Taiwan | Genre: Musical | Run Time: 1:46 | Language: Chinese, English

There are many diseases exist in the world, either physically or psychologically, explicit or hidden, and there are many people suffering. We might keep away from them because we don't understand their pain. Although they need a lot of help and tolerance, they are not worse then others. Instead, they have their own advantages, they have unique behavior and expression. Their existences are like special and lovely melody.
