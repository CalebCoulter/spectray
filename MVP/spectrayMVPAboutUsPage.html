About Us

Christopher Thomas, CEO

Christopher Thomas is a filmmaker with several years of experience in videography and editing. During the summer of 2015, he created a sketch comedy web series called "The Exchange Street Show" that was featured in several online film festivals. He graduated with a degree in mass media production from the University of Akron in December 2015. In his spare time, Chris likes to rescue cats from trees, sing Frank Sinatra songs and put too much strawberry jam on his PB&Js.

Joseph Mario Micale, Chief Marketing Officer

Joseph Mario Micale is responsible for making others look good through his marketing and communications professionalism as well as his work as a freelance producer, videographer and editor. His marketing experience began with his student internship in 2014 for the communications and marketing department at the University of Akron, while his video-crating prowess began in grade school. When he is not working on a way to entertain and engage an audience through his viral videos or bigger picture ideas; he enjoys dancing awkwardly to music, playing games, and savoring buffalo chicken dip.
